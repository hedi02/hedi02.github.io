Mathematically fairer school zoning

Wikipedia: In mathematics, a Voronoi diagram is a partitioning of a plane into regions based on distance to points in a specific subset of the plane. That set of points (called seeds, sites, or generators) is specified beforehand, and for each seed there is a corresponding region consisting of all points closer to that seed than to any other.

Voronoi diagram, dan turunannya, banyak digunakan dalam perancangan zonasi sekolah. Contoh: Kota Melbourne, http://melbourneschoolzones.com

Peta sekolah diambil dari http://sekolah.data.kemdikbud.go.id
