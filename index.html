Mathematically fairer school zoning

Wikipedia: In mathematics, a Voronoi diagram is a partitioning of a plane into regions based on distance to points in a specific subset of the plane. That set of points (called seeds, sites, or generators) is specified beforehand, and for each seed there is a corresponding region consisting of all points closer to that seed than to any other. These regions are called Voronoi cells.
Voronoi diagram, dan turunannyanya, banyak digunakan dalam perancangan zonasi sekolah di luar negeri. Contoh: Kota Melbourne, http://melbourneschoolzones.com
